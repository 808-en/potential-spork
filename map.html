Seedmap Explorer Guide

Welcome to the ultimate Seedmap app! This tool compiles all complex spatial coordinate logic directly inside an immersive interactive mapping canvas.

Controls

  • Left Click & Drag to Pan the world.
  • Mouse Wheel to Zoom in and out.
  • Double Click on any point on the map to place a Custom Waypoint.

Diamond & Moonstone Finder

Enable "Diamonds" or "Moonstone" finder modes. When active, deep ore structures will be sequentially calculated for the given Seed and plotted onto the grid. Click any generated ore point to view its target Y-layer depths!

Measure Tool

Click "Measure Distance" to turn on ruler markers. Click multiple locations on the map to see real-time vector path tracking with accurate voxel measurements.
